How the score works

Go Window rates every daylight hour from 0 to 100 for your sport, then finds the 2–4 hour stretch with the most consistently high scores — that's your window.

Cycling cares most about wind and gusts, then rain, temperature and UV. Running weighs dew point (how muggy it feels), heat, rain, air quality and pollen. Swimming looks at water temperature, official water-quality rating, waves, wind and air temperature.

Bands: Prime 80+, Good 60+, OK 40+, Poor 20+, Avoid below 20. The score is guidance, not a guarantee — always use your own judgement.
